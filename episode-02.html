Episode 02

Law School & Imposter Syndrome

Imposters syndrome is something that many people struggle with, regardless of age or experience. But sometimes spaces that have a traditional archetype can make these feelings more intense for those who may not fit the mould. Coupled with the hype and prestige of law school, navigating new spaces, new norms, and unspoken rules can feel absolutely unbearable. In this episode, we’re talking about imposter’s syndrome from three different vantage points. Like, can we get an outline or summary for how to achieve this “confidence” we’ve been hearing about?
